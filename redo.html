Collaborative Projects

angelsguard.me

Collaborated in a team to create angelsguard.me, a web app designed to efficiently and discretely send help to a people in need at public events; with a click of a button, security at the events will be promptly alerted through text that the user needs assistance and locate them based on the profile description.

Developers: Edwin Finch (Back-end and Front-end), Annie Liang (Front-end), Sangeet Parashar (Back-end) and Jason Lee (Back-end)
